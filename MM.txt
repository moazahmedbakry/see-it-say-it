Human Rights Council Committee
Subject: Modern Slavery
Sponsor: [Italy]

The Human Rights Council,

Worried about the growing problem of modern slavery, like forced labor, child exploitation, and human trafficking, in both rich and poor countries,

Asks all countries to take action right away to stop modern slavery by:
a) Making stronger laws and better enforcement,
b) Helping survivors with support and legal help;

Encourages countries and organizations to work together by:
a) Sharing information to stop human trafficking networks,
b) Supporting each other in fighting modern slavery around the world.
